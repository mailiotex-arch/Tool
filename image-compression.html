How It Works

Our image compression tool uses advanced algorithms to reduce your image file size while maintaining visual quality. Here's how to use it:

Step 1: Upload Your Images

Drag and drop your images into the upload area or click to browse your files. You can upload up to 20 images at once (5MB each).

Step 2: Adjust Compression Settings

Use the slider to select your desired compression level (0 = maximum compression, 100 = minimum compression). Choose your preferred output format or keep the original format.

Step 3: Compress and Download

Click the "Compress Images" button. Once processing is complete, you'll see a comparison of original and compressed file sizes with download links.
